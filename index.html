Who We Are

Based on Ottawa, Ontario, Cat Rescue Network (CRN) is a non-profit, volunteer driven organization established in 1999. With no headquarters or overhead, we put all of our time and funds into finding homes for cats in need.

Making a Difference

Cat Rescue Network's motto is "Until there are none, save one." We live out this motto by responding to emergency situations and taking in cats off the street, who go directly into foster homes.

How You Can Help

Consider volunteering or donating to CRN to help cats in need.

As well, if you are interested in fostering a cat in need, please contact us directly at info@cr.com.
